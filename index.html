To play the game, use your hands as "portals" to suck in basketballs flying across the screen and transport them so that they will go in the hoop! Your camera must be turned on for the game to recognize your hands, and if a basketball is "in" one of them it will be immediately transported to the other. The speed and direction of the basketball will be kept the same after transporting, and your goal is to get the basketballs into one of the two hoops. Good luck!
